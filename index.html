About Me

Hi, I'm Filip Bogusłowicz, also known as SonosVitae. I specialize in composing immersive, natural-sounding music that bridges the gap between your vision and the final product. As a classically trained percussionist and sound designer, my philosophy is that game music (whether instrumental or electronic) must fully immerse the player in the world they inhabit. My style ranges from atmospheric, dark, organic electronic textures to majestic, grand orchestral scores.

I have several notable projects under my belt: the highly praised, jazzy soundtrack for the detective visual novel Beyond R: Rule Ripper, the unsettling horror-centric music for the Isaac: Epiphany mod, and my current work on Drehmal: Apotheosis (blending live instruments with massive electronic soundscapes). With experience including three scores performed live at the Białystok Philharmonic Hall, I provide a full-service audio experience from the initial arrangement to the final mix and master.
